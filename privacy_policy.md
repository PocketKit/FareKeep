# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 29 March 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **FareKeep**. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and tax-related information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Our App is designed to provide tax and logbook solutions primarily for users in **Australia**, as well as foreign individuals who pay tax or GST to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that your data is handled strictly in compliance with relevant privacy regulations, as well as the App Store and Google Play Store policies.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The type of information we gather depends on your use of the App:

- **Personal and Profile Information:** Information you provide, such as your business profile details, name, and email address (e.g., when signing in via Google Sign-In).
- **Logbook and Tax Data:** Data regarding your trips, vehicle types, logbooks, and transactions (expenses, income, and payment methods). This data is strictly used to help you calculate and maintain tax records.
- **Automatically Collected Data:** As with many mobile apps, we may automatically collect diagnostic data, device information, and usage statistics to improve application performance.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect strictly to operate, maintain, and provide the features of the App, including:
- **Tax & ATO Compliance Management:** To accurately calculate trip distances, generate GST/tax reports, and maintain logbooks that align with Australian Taxation Office (ATO) standards.
- **Backup and Sync:** To securely back up your data to your authorized cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive) so you never lose your tax records.
- **Improving the App:** To analyze usage trends and fix user-reported bugs.

*Disclaimer: While our App is built to assist in maintaining records that are compatible with ATO rules and regulations, the accuracy of the data entered and the final tax lodgment remains the sole responsibility of the user. For more info on ATO compliance, please visit the [official ATO website](https://www.ato.gov.au).*

## 4. Third-Party Libraries and Services

Our App integrates robust third-party services to enhance functionality and ensure data safety. These third-party providers have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information:
- **Google Play Services / Apple App Store:** Used for app distribution, updates, and in-app purchases (if applicable).
- **Firebase & Google Sign-In:** Used for secure user authentication and error reporting. [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google Drive API:** Used specifically for the App's user-driven data backup feature. Our App only accesses the specific backup files it creates to ensure your tax data remains strictly under your control.

## 5. User Consent and Data Retention

- **Consent:** We process your personal and tax data only with your explicit consent, which is granted when you use the App or connect third-party accounts (like Google Drive).
- **Data Retention & Storage:** Most of your sensitive tax and trip data is stored locally on your device using a secure SQLite database. If you choose to use the backup feature, the data is encrypted and sent directly to your personal cloud storage (user's personal Google Drive). We do not store your private logbook entries on our own external servers.
- **Data Deletion:** You can delete your data at any time by clearing the App's data from your device settings or using the provided in-app tools to erase logbooks and profiles.

## 6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. We implement commercially acceptable security standards to protect your locally stored data and ensure that any cloud communication relies on robust, secure protocols (HTTPS/OAuth). However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We may also notify you via forced updates or in-app alerts if substantial changes occur to our data handling practices. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
